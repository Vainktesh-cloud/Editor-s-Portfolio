About Vainky

Hello! I'm Vainktesh Tiwari, also known as Vainky, a professional video editor with over two years of dedicated experience in bringing creative visions to life. I have successfully edited more than 500 videos, combining both short and long-form content for various platforms.

My journey in video editing has allowed me to collaborate with a diverse range of talented creators, including Vivanyaarr, Sarcastic Monk, and Ayushmaan Bhav, among many others. I am proficient in Adobe Premiere Pro (2 years of experience), have a beginner's grasp of Adobe After Effects, and am skilled in Adobe Photoshop for graphic elements and touch-ups.

I am passionate about transforming raw footage into compelling narratives, ensuring every edit enhances the story and captivates the audience. My expertise spans various genres and platforms, always striving for excellence and innovation in every project.
